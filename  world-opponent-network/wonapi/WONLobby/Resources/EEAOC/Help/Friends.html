Friends List

You can use the Friends Lists to keep track of your online buddies, and you can have up to 255 users on it at any given time. Online friends are sorted to the top of the list. Offline friends are displayed as disabled text and sorted to the bottom. Friends that are currently in a game will have the In Game Icon displayed next to their name.

There are 4 buttons at the bottom of the friends list: Add (add a friend), Del (delete a friend), > (locate a friend), and >> (locate all friends).

Detecting Friends

Detecting your friends depends on whether they are on your Lobby server or on a different Lobby server. Friends that join, leave, or enter games on your server will have their statuses in the Friends List updated automatically. Friends on other servers will not. You can check the status of friends on other servers at any time by selecting the Refresh (>>) button at the bottom of the Friends List.

Adding/Deleting Friends

There are several ways to add friends. You may click on the Add button at the bottom of the Friends Lists; this will display a dialog that allows you to enter a user name to add. You may right-click on a user name in a User List and select Add to Friends. Or you may left-click on a user name in a Chat Window and select Add to Friends.

There are several ways to delete friends. You may select a name in the Friends List and click on the Del button at the bottom. You may right-click on a name in the Friends List and select Remove from Friends. You may right-click on a user name in a User List and select Remove from Friends. Or you may left-click on a user name in a Chat Window and select Remove from Friends.

Locating Friends

Locating Friends searches the Sierra Online servers. There are two ways to locate a friend. You may select a friend in the Friends list and click the Locate (>) button below. Or you can right-click on a friend in the Friends List and select Locate.

When you locate friends, their statuses are put in the Chat Window to the left of the Friends List. If they are on your server, a message/link to their current room and the game they are playing (if they are in a game) will be displayed. If they are on another server, a message/link to that server will be displayed. If they are not online, a message to that effect will be displayed.
